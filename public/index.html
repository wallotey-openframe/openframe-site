03 / Studio

A few things about us.

Open Frame Media is an independent, Accra-based production house crafting films for organizations, cultural institutions, and innovators who have something meaningful to say.

We combine over 12 years of industry experience across our core team, working in a distinct register marked by patient cinematography, authentic subjects, and an active philosophy of listening rather than over‑narrating.

That same attentiveness shapes how we work. From the first conversation, we partner closely with our clients – attentive to detail and committed to capturing their vision. The person you meet at kickoff remains involved throughout the project, ensuring consistent communication and a strong connection. Our deliberate craft guarantees that every project reflects our high standards and the client's best interests.

With 15+ completed projects and a record of 100% client satisfaction, we've partnered with organizations, cultural institutions, and innovators to craft films that resonate.

Every piece of work is one we'd proudly watch ourselves. That's the standard that keeps us selective – and the reason our films linger in memory long after the credits fade.
